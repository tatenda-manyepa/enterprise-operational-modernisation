# Business Context & Current State Assessment

## Overview

This document provides the initial business context and operational assessment for Apex Operational Systems Ltd as part of the Cloud Transformation & Operational Modernisation Programme.

The assessment identifies:

- Current operational challenges
- Infrastructure limitations
- Business risks
- Resilience gaps
- Strategic business objectives

---

## Key Focus Areas

- Operational stability
- High availability & disaster recovery
- Reactive support limitations
- On-premises infrastructure constraints
- Scalability challenges
- Monitoring & observability gaps
