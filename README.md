## Key Architecture Principles

### Reliability First

Operational continuity is prioritised through resilient architecture patterns, monitoring, backup strategies, and disaster recovery planning.

### Security by Design

Security controls are embedded throughout the platform using identity management, secrets protection, audit logging, and governance controls.

### Operational Excellence

The platform is designed to improve visibility, reduce manual effort, and support faster incident response.

### Automation Where Valuable

Automation is introduced to reduce repetitive operational tasks and improve consistency.

### Business-Aligned Transformation

Technical decisions are driven by business objectives, operational outcomes, and long-term sustainability.

## Fictional Client

### Apex Operational Systems Ltd

A mid-sized enterprise operating a legacy operational platform supporting high-volume fulfilment and operational workflows.

The organisation is experiencing recurring operational instability, limited observability, manual recovery processes, and increasing operational complexity.

The Cloud Transformation & Operational Modernisation Programme was established to improve resilience, scalability, operational efficiency, and long-term business sustainability.
